Accessible HTML

The first practical step towards accessibility is to create valid HTML, which better provides a consistent experience across all browsers and assistive technologies.

Natura11y's premade examples have been validated through the W3C Nu Html Checker. It is a good idea to periodically check your work here, too at different stages of development.

Color Contrast

Contrast ratio refers the difference in color between text and background color or image. When applying your own colors to our color system, check the color-contrast ratio to ensure readability. To be compliant with WCAG Level AA, aim for a contrast ratio of 4.5:1 or higher.

In the examples above, the dark text on green passes readability with a color contrast ratio of 7.14, while the white text on green fails with a color contrast ratio of 2.09.

Learn how our color system helps you keep your accessible colors consistent throughout your layouts.

Aria

Accessible Rich Internet Applications (WAI-ARIA) roles are present throughout Natura11y's components. Some are added with Natura11y's Javascript, while others are present within the example markup. Still others you will need to add yourself, as needed.

Focus Management

Natura11y provides consistent and noticable focus indicators for those who navigate with the keyboard.
